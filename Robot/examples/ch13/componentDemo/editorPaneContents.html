TextView

This view shows a number of ways to capture and display text:

  • The first field can capture one line of arbitrary text.
  • The second field does not display the input and is typically used for accepting passwords.
  • The third will only accept integers.
  • The fourth accepts dates in the form mm/dd/yy.
  • The fifth accepts larger amounts of text.

Be sure to experiment with clicking the mouse in the fields, hitting "Enter" after typing text, and hitting "Tab" after typing text.
